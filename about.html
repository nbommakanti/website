About Dr. Nikhil Bommakanti, M.D.

A Commitment to Excellence in Retinal Care

Philosophy of Care

Dr. Nikhil Bommakanti is a board-certified ophthalmologist and a fellowship-trained vitreoretinal surgeon. He believes that the best patient outcomes are achieved through a combination of advanced medical knowledge, state-of-the-art technology, and a strong doctor-patient relationship built on trust and open communication. He takes the time to thoroughly explain complex conditions and treatment options, empowering patients to be active participants in their own care.

Education & Training

  • Vitreoretinal Surgery Fellowship: Wills Eye Hospital, Philadelphia, PA
  • Ophthalmology Residency: Kellogg Eye Center, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI
  • Medical School: Columbia University Vagelos College of Physicians and Surgeons, New York, NY
  • Research Fellowship: Oxford University, Oxford, UK
  • Undergraduate: B.S. in Bioengineering, summa cum laude, University of Illinois at Chicago, Chicago, IL

Professional Affiliations

Dr. Bommakanti is an active member of the American Academy of Ophthalmology (AAO) and the American Society of Retina Specialists (ASRS).
