Philosophy of Care

Conditions

Comprehensive care for a wide range of retinal diseases including:

Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD)

A leading cause of vision loss in older adults, affecting central vision. Advanced treatments such as intravitreal injections may be offered to manage both wet and dry AMD.

Diabetic Retinopathy

A serious complication of diabetes that can cause severe vision loss. Laser treatments, injections, and/or complex surgery may be performed to preserve sight.

Macular Holes & Puckers

Conditions that distort central vision. Vitrectomy surgery may be recommended to repair the macula.

Retinal Tears & Detachment

Structural damage to the retina which may result in vision loss. In-office laser procedures and/or advanced vitreoretinal surgery are performed to repair the retina.

Retinal Vascular Occlusions

Blockages in the veins or arteries of the retina. Treatment may involve injections to reduce swelling.

Uveitis & Ocular Inflammatory Disease

Inflammation inside the eye that can damage delicate tissues. Treatment may involve medications or injections to control inflammation.
